Mochi Lang is a Lisp dialect, trying to follow Clojure syntax, that compiled down to Go lang. At this point, it doesn’t looks like Clojure yet.

It isn’t a language on its own, It’s a frontend for Go lang, at the bottom level, Go lang is invoked to compile your code.
